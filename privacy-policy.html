Privacy Policy

Designcharm is committed to protecting the privacy of its website visitors. This Privacy Policy outlines the types of information we collect, how it is used, and how it is protected.



Information Collection:

Designcharm collects the following information from website visitors:

  • Name

  • Email address

This information is collected when visitors sign up for our design subscription services for jewelry and diamond businesses.


Cookies:

Designcharm does not use cookies on its website.


Third-Party Services:

Designcharm uses Google Analytics to track website visitors and monitor site usage. This information is used to improve our website and provide better services to our visitors.


Accounts and Login:

Designcharm does not allow users to create accounts or log in to our website.


Handling User Data Requests:

Designcharm does not have a system for handling user data requests, such as accessing or deleting information.


Changes to This Privacy Policy:

Designcharm reserves the right to make changes to this Privacy Policy at any time. Any changes to this Privacy Policy will be posted on this page and will take effect immediately.


Contact Us:

If you have any questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at Email: harshl.design@gmail.com
